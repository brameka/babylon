I'm on a mission to put an end to mum/mom shame for the average mum like me...So here's an app with 27 easy, short, non-messy and non-annoying things to teach your kid before starting school. Now go forth and do bare minimum

Tips:

  • Repeat activities as many times as you need to master the skills
  • Once your kid becomes confident in the skills it builds their self esteem
  • If your kid doesn't like an activity, just move on and try again later
  • Always praise your kids effort
  • Most importantly have fun
